About Sentinel

Sentinel is a smart campus security solution designed to keep students and security personnel connected in real time. Many students experience or witness security threats but hesitate to report them due to fear, lengthy processes, or uncertainty about whom to contact. Sentinel eliminates these barriers by providing a fast, anonymous, and efficient way to report incidents and access emergency assistance.

By making safety swift, smart, and seamless, Sentinel is not just a response tool it’s a preventative solution that empowers students and ensures a more secure learning environment.
